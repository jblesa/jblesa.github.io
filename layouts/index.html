About

I am a software developer and embedded systems engineer based in Madrid, with a PhD in Telecommunications. I have over 15 years of experience designing software for UAV autopilots, sensor networks, and safety-critical embedded platforms.

I currently serve as Director & Head of Software and IT at UAV Navigation (Grupo Oesía), where I lead the engineering teams responsible for flight control systems and ground software.
